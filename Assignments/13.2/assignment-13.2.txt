## **Quiz**

- What is the difference between ***var*** and ***let***?
	let can not be redeclared and it is block scoped where as
	var can be reassigned and redeclared and is function scoped
	var also is hoisted and is declared before assignment 
- What is the difference between **var** and ***const***?
	const can not be reassigned or redeclared and is block scoped like let
- What is the difference between **let** and **const**?
	let is able to be reassigned and const can't
- What is hoisting?
	hoisting is referring to the declaration before assignments. In the 
	instance of var it will declare the variable in the beginning but 
	wont have an assignment until it runs through the code.
